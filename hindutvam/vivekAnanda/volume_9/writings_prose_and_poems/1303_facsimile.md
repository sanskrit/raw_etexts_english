## FACSIMILE

![](facsimile.jpg)

One circle more the spiral path of life ascends  
And Time's restless shuttle running

back and fro

through maze of warp and woof spins out a

stronger piece.

RAILROAD STATION,

W. U. TELEGRAPH AND                                      P. O.
STONERIDGE.

NATIONAL EXPRESS OFFICES,                                          
ULSTER CO., N. Y.

BINNEWATER, ULSTER CO., N. Y.
