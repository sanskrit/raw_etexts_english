## CXLI

ALMORA,  
*20th May, 1898*.

DEAR MARGOT (Margaret E. Noble or Sister Nivedita.)

. . . Duty has no end, and the world is extremely selfish.

Be of good cheer. "Never a worker of good came to grief." . . . 

Ever yours etc.,

VIVEKANANDA.
