## LV

*To Mr. Francis H. Leggett*

54 W. 33RD ST.  
NEW YORK  
THE 4TH MAY '95

DEAR FRIEND,

Many thanks for your kind present. The cigars are indeed delicious — and
a hundred times so, as coming from you.

With everlasting love and regards,

I remain yours truly,  
VIVEKANANDA
