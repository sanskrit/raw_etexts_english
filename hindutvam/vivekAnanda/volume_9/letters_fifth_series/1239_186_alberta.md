## CLXXXVI

*To Miss Alberta Sturges*

*\[Swami Vivekananda sent the following postcard.\]*

\[CONSTANTINOPLE,

*November 1, 1900*\]

DEAR ALBERTA,

How are you? I am having a grand Turkish time.

Yours,  
VIVEKANANDA
