## LXXXVI

LONDON,  
*21st Nov., 1895.*

DEAR MRS. BULL,

 

I sail by the *Britannic* on Wednesday, the 27th. My work so far has
been very satisfactory here and I am sure to do splendid work here next
summer. . . . 

Yours with love,

VIVEKANANDA.
