## LXXIX

*To Mrs. Ole Bull*

228 W. 39

NEW YORK

*24 December 1895*

Merry Christmas and happy New Year to you, dear Mrs. Bull. And may peace
and health rest on you and yours for ever. I am going out of town today
and will be back in ten days.

My love to all.

Yours affectionately,

VIVEKANANDA
