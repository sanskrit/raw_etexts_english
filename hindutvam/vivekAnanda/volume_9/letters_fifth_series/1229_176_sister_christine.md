## CLXXVI

*To Sister Christine*

*\[On July 3, 1900, before departing for Detroit with Swami Turiyananda
and Miss Minnie Boock, Swami Vivekananda dispatched a telegram.\]*

\[POSTMARKED: NEW YORK

*July 3, 1900*\]

STARTED REACH TOMORROW WEDNESDAY 2 P.M. COME STATION WABASH.

SWAMI VIVEKANANDA
