## CXI

*To Mrs. Francis Leggett*

ALMORA

*20 June '97*

DEAR MOTHER —

Herewith I take the liberty to introduce to you Miss Tremayne of London,
a particular friend of mine going over to the States.

Any help given to her would greatly oblige.

Yours in the Lord,  
VIVEKANANDA
