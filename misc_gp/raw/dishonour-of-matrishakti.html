॥ Shri Hari॥

Dishonour of Matrishakti

मन जाने सब बात, जान बूझ अवगुण करे।

क्यों चाहत कुसलात, कर दीपक कूएँ पड़े॥

‘Nothing is hidden to mind. Mind knows everything and knowingly resorts to evils. Then, expects his welfare, it is like this that with an earthen lamp in hand he falls into a well not noticing it.’

At present, such heinous and dreadful sins are being committed that make our hair stand on end, eyes are overflowed with tears, heart pities to hear them. Oh! Ram, Ram-Ram, what a great injustice, what severe crimes you are committing! But you have no thought towards it! The human species has been described as the rarest—

दुर्लभो मानुषो देहो देहिनां क्षणभङ्गुर:।

(Shrimadbha. 11/2/29)

‘The human body is a rare boon to embodied souls and that too is very transient.’

लब्ध्वा सुदुर्लभमिदं बहुसम्भवान्ते

मानुष्यमर्थदमनित्यमपीह धीर:।

(Shrimadbha. 11/9/29)

‘Having after many births in this world acquired the rare human body, however frail, which is still the means of attaining the object of life, a wise man should speedily strive to attain Liberation, before it gets destroyed body.’

बड़ें भाग मानुष तनु पावा।

सुर दुर्लभ सब ग्रंथन्हि गावा॥

(Manas 7/42/7)

‘It is by good fortune that you have secured a human body, which, as declared by all the scriptures, is difficult even for the gods to attain.’

What a severe crime, what a great sin, what a great injustice it is with the beings to end in the beginning of this rare human body or to cut it! My mind is afire, I am much afflicted, but what to do? This is the climax of sin to destroy it or not to let this human body be born, which is capable to attain the Supreme Lord. Man has girded up his loins that a living being may not get the rare human body, one may not attain the highest good, one may not emancipate oneself so one may not even develop of foetus, it is to be destroyed by taking medicine earlier, it is to be aborted, it is to be destroyed by cutting it, it is to be destroyed by miscarriage, it is to be destroyed by forced abortion, it is to be destroyed by foeticide; it may incur great sin, we may go to hell but we cannot give opportunity to any one to redeem oneself—man is bent upon to do so. Now, what should I do? To whom should I speak? And who is to listen to me? None pays attention.

For the saints like us, it has been ordained in the Shastras not to wander about in the four months of rainy season. We stay at one place for two months; a few stay for three months and a few stay for four months at a place and they don’t move about. There is reason behind it is that it rains in the four months, every seed sprouts. After sprouting it becomes plant and then it becomes a big tree. By the movement, the germinated seeds are crushed with our feet and they are ruined. So we stop our movements in the rainy season, so that violence may not be caused to anyone. It is said in the Bhagawat—‘If violence is to be avoided, the movement of the body should be minimised.’—‘हिंसा कायाद्यनीहया’ (7/15/23). If there is so much consideration of even the immoveable creatures that the movement in the rainy season has been forbidden, then what is to talk of the mobile beings? But today the people are making efforts to destroy the human body, even superior to gods, what will be their fate?

It is not my sense of pride that I say but it is what I have heard or what I have understood which I say that as much effort the Indian culture has made for the emancipation of the living beings, so much has not been made by any other culture. Has any religious sect—Christianity, Islam, Yahudi, Buddhism, Parasi etc., made so much effort for the welfare of living beings? What is that particular religious sect whose foundation is only for the redemption of living beings? You, yourself may analyse. They make efforts to increase their number and to preach their faith undoubtedly, but they never make any effort for the highest good of the living beings. The culture which is meant only for the salvation of the living beings, not allow any being to come on the earth, check the soul from the birth or to destroy it, how horrible sin it is? It is a matter of wonder that this vice is being considered as a feature of modern civilization in the society. It means clearly to go to the hell as soon as possible, to suffer in the most terrible hell and if there is any impediment in going to hell this way then, it will be a tyranny.

Just think over, is it good to check the progress of anyone? Will you not let any one to become rich if he wants to become rich, if any one wants to become righteous, will you not let him to be so, if one wants to redeem oneself, would you not allow him to redeem, will you check anyone to be physically strong, stout and robust, is it a virtue? What a great injustice and tyranny for our petty comforts it is to destroy the other souls who might follow the path of God-realization and not to let them take the birth! Don’t commit sin knowingly, O the bread-giver! Be kind to the least. I belong to you, I have been brought up by you and still I depend on you for my livelihood. You are my mother and father! Be kind enough not to commit such a heinous crime. There is a reference in Parasharasmriti—

यत्पापं ब्रह्महत्याया द्विगुणं गर्भपातने।

प्रायश्चित्तं न तस्यास्ति तस्यास्त्यागो विधीयते॥

(4/20)

‘Sin of committing abortion is double to the murder of a Brahmana. There is no atonement of this great sin of doing abortion, there is the moral rule to abandon such a woman and not to accept her as his wife.’

If you are interested in birth control, observe celibacy. I recommend it to you. You will not be the victim of sin if you follow the rule of celibacy, rather your physique will be healthy, free from any disease and strong. But to ruin the body and not to bear the child is a matter of shame. How shameful and sad it is!—

श्रूयतां धर्मसर्वस्वं श्रुत्वा चैवावधार्यताम्।

आत्मन: प्रतिकूलानि परेषां न समाचरेत्॥

Listen essence of the religions and follow it. The conduct and behaviours that do not suit your conscience, should not be behaved with others. Will it be evil or virtue if anybody creates hindrance in your progress, checks your birth, checks your flourish, checks your study, checks your divine adoration or check you to achieve your goal? Just consider! A being is coming to the form of a human birth and to become the hurdle of the way and not to let it be born, what a great sin it is! Alright, if you practise celibacy, your body will remain healthy, neither you will be sinner nor will you be condemned and contrary to it you will enjoy a sound physique also. But you don’t want to give up worldly enjoyments, you will indulge into sense-enjoyments and decay your health and body but not to let the issue be born—it is a matter of great downfall.

You don’t hesitate to kill the small creatures. They cannot oppose you; but is there no justice in the court of the Lord? What a great evil it is to destroy the weak and helpless? Hear and read the fable of Mahabharat. In the war period the enemy is made to be alert and to be cautious before the arrow is shot at. It is a great torture and injustice to destroy or harm such small creatures or insects who are too poor and helpless to do anything, they cannot even escape themselves and they cannot harm you even. Gentlemen! Pay heed to my words, just think. You don’t want your ill then what right have you got to do ill to others? Don’t disturb anybody’s happiness, don’t be hurdle in any body’s prosperity, don’t create problem in anybody’s birth, let it be good of others, don’t disturb. Don’t want to do such behaviour with others what does not suit to you, yourself. This is the first morality.

The creatures that are incapable to oppose, should not be tortured, to torture them is not tolerable even to the Lord. For those who are powerful to ruin others, the Gita has mentioned them ‘demons’—‘प्रभवन्त्युग्रकर्माण: क्षयाय जगतोऽहिता:’ (16/9). Knowingly, you commit the murder of helpless and dumb creatures and want your welfare; how is it possible? How will be your the highest good? I want whole-heartedly that you should not meet the evil fate, you should get deliverance! But what should I do?

What is another culture that teaches the way of spiritual advancement so much as Indian culture? The Christians, the Muslims work to enhence their majority that the number of the adherents of their religious sect may be large, they may earn repute. But who has got the keen desire to work for the highest good the entire living beings, who is there to work for their emancipation, to get them rid of the misery, hell, birth and death and to make them able to get the Supreme bliss forever? Who performs the Yagyas etc., for the universal peace and redemption? I saw a pamphlet in Delhi. It was written in the pamphlet to kill him who is found doing idol-worship. What injustice is done by them who believe in idol-worship? What sin do they commit? Whom do they assail any harm? What disturbance do they create for you by their idol-worship? Now, it is you to judge and speak which religious sect is there that talks of the redemption of beings?

उमा संत कइ इहइ बड़ाई।

मंद करत जो करइ भलाई॥

(Manas, 5/40/7)

Said Shiva, “Uma, here lies the greatness of a saint, who returns good for evil.”

What a great tyranny is there in that culture where there is restriction of a soul to be born and for this purpose there is the provision of vasectomy in the very begining, abortion and the surgical operation? If any being is about to become a man and that is restrained, that being will be born as heretic who doesn’t have faith in the Hindu culture and he will ruin the Hindus. If the number of heretics increase, what will they do? Which is that religious sect where the effort of the welfare of the entire living beings is made? It is only the Gita where lies the sermon that even the fierce war may bring you the highest good! Even the war of Mahabharat was fought on the holy land of ‘Kurukshetra’ because the death there leads to salvation. The war, too, should be fought on that place where the death may result in deliverance. Thus, if the culture that talks always of the highest good in all the circumstances is banned for the birth of the mankind, what a great crime it is! I am not talking in vanity but it is an expression of pride, so I ask for pardon. This is the assembly of spiritual- seekers, it has been organised for nine days regular and complete recitation of ‘Ramcharitmanas’—is there any such ceremonial assembly organised among the Christians, Muslims, Jews or Parasies for the welfare of all the living beings? If it so happens, tell me, what is the motive behind this ‘Assembly’ and the reciting of the Ramayan? What is the objective of all this, whether it is to increase our followers, or to collect money or to get fame and glory, or for the honour and name or to get worldly enjoyments or for the progress of the world, what is the purpose?

Ramji did not do any harm even to his enemy—‘अरिहुक अनभल कीन्ह न रामा’ (Manas 2/183/6). When He sent Angad to Ravan, he said to him also as may advance my cause and serve his welfare at the same time—‘काजु हमार तासु हित होई’ (6/17/8). He sends the messenger to him also, who has kidnapped Sitaji and he is ready to kill him or himself to be killed. With a view that his purpose may be served, he may get back his wife but Ravan’s welfare might be served, which is that culture where such an instance may be quoted? What a great injustice it is to restrain a being to be born in such a culture? You are committing that great sin very rapidily which you know and which has been declared a great sin by the Shastras and yet you want redemption, how is it possible?

There were two Jats. The both had cows, buffaloes, sheep, goats etc., in abundance thus, the both were very rich. Their farming was also much luxuriant. The woman of one family was hostile to the other family. She used to burn his grass going to his field, damaged his crops, but in spite of her evildoing, her sons, grandsons, cows and buffaloes were prospering leaps and bounds. The husband of that woman used to say—‘There is great injustice in the God’s court.’ She does ill of others but prospers well. Many years she kept evil doing passed. Now, the young sons of that woman began to die, her wealth was lost, her farming also came to an end. Then, he said—‘There is no injustice in the Lord’s court but there is delay.’ Really, there is no delay also. By the dispensation of the Almighty, whatever should be done, is being done at the appropriate time. Man’s apprehension is defective so it looks to him that it is delayed.

Most of the people doubt that righteous people are suffering and those who commit vices are prospering, living comfortably and enjoying pleasure. But it is a mystery, the result will make it clear. Then, there will be nothing except lamenting.

सो परत्र दुख पावइ सिर धुनि धुनि पछिताइ।

कालहि कर्महि ईस्वरहि मिथ्या दोस लगाइ॥

(Manas, 7/43)

“He suffers torture in the other world and beats his head in remorse, wrongly attributing the blame on time, fate and God.”

So, it is better to be cautious earlier. At least, knowingly don’t do great sin and act of tyranny.

When the Pandavas lived in the forest, Draupadi asked a question to Yudhisthir—‘Maharaj! You don’t move even a single step against morality, you never hurt anyone, you never assail any harm to anyone, but even then, you are wandering about in the forest, you have nothing edible with you, you depend on the wild fruits and roots to live your life. But, Duryodhan who is always unjust and immoral, is ruling and enjoying the life. Then, does righteousness and moral conduct pay or not? Does the Lord do justice or not?’ Yudhisthir said—Devi! How did this idea crop in your mind? The person who follows morality for his own joy and comforts, does not know the quintensence of religion. Actually, he alone knows the essence of religion who does not give up morality despite his adversities. The comforts and enjoyments have no value, righteousness is valuable. The importance of worldly comforts is there in the animals also. I don’t follow morality to get the worldly pleasures. Now, consider that if even the morality is not followed for the comforts and enjoyment, then what will be the condition of those who are committing the crime of abortion for the sake of their own joys and comforts? What will be more severe sin that they kill the girls particularily lying in the pregnency by the abortion?

I have seen written on the board—‘Test to know whether there is son or daughter in the womb.’ It means that if it is girl, abort it. The men have been given more importance than the women in the world. But the glory of the Matrishakti is much more than the glory of father. The position of the mother has been accepted thousand times higher than that of the father.

सहस्रं तु पितॄन्माता गौरवेणातिरिच्यते॥

(Manu, 2/145)

First, mother’s name is taken and then after comes father’s name. For example—Radheshyam, Sitaram, Lakshminarayan, Gaurishanker etc. The abortion of the female child is the great insult of Matrishakti. All these brothers and sisters sitting here have come from the mothers lap and not from father’s lap. All have been brought up by mothers and not by fathers. All have been suckled by mothers and not by fathers. When the calf drinks milk, the cow fondles him by licking, she looks towards him that makes him hale and hearty. If he is made to drink the milk of an other cow he will not be so healthy. The reason behind is that the child is brought up by the love and affection of the mother and it is not only her milk. The mother has the extraordinary significance. For the sake of your own joys and comforts you ruin the Matrishakti. If you effect abortion in the womb itself and don’t allow to be born, then how will you be born? It is a great sin to effect the abortion of a female child. There can be no more insult of the Matrishakti than this. The girl who is in the womb is the Matrishakti, if you effect abortion of the female child, whose milk will you drink? Where will you get the lap? How will you get the affection and love of a mother? Who will embrace you to her breast?

The scholar of Kashi Madanmohanji Maharaj used to say that once, he had gone to attend someone’s marriage-party. The sisters and mothers had come there well dressed up with valuable and decent garments and ornaments. One sister had a child in her lap. That child happened to pass stool. The sisters sitting near her said to her that her child was passing stool. Just hearing, she said—‘O, keep quiet, don’t make a noise, my child’s passing of stool will be hindered.’ Tell me, where is such an emotional feeling except a mother? Her fine silken sari is being spoiled but she has no care for it but she has the care of her child that if he is not able to pass his stool, he may fall ill. What a remarkable emotional feeling it is! Such an insult and disgrace of that Matrishakti that her pregnancy is destroyed through abortion! Who will bear us in womb? Nowadays, the discussion about the equality and similarity of the men’s and women’s right is going on, then, men should also be pregnant like women. Actually, the women have no equal right to the men but contrary to it, they have more right than men and they are superior to men because they are mothers verily mothers. The saints have many houses but they are not called homes. The homes are designated because of women only. That is why the Shastras dictate—

न गृहं गृहमित्याहुर्ग्रहिणी गृहमुच्यते।

(Maha. Shanti. 144/6)

‘It means, the house is not a home, a woman is called home.’ A man buys a nose-ring and a Petticoat in the market, if he is asked whether he wears the nose-ring, he will say ‘No’, it is needed at the home. It is the ‘Grih-Lakshmi’ that is real home. When the daughter-in-law comes, the sisters and mothers say that the daughter-in-law has come, now, it is her home. The people boast that the women have been insulted too much, now, they are being honoured by us. But in reality, as much the womankind are being insulted now, so much have they never been insulted in the past. Only to protect the dignity of woman, the Mahabharat war was fought. Ramchandra fought war against Ravan for the protection of womankind. For the protection of womankind only, Bhishmji gave up his life, but did not shoot arrow at Shikhandi (who was first a woman not in the present). But today, the Matrishakti is being crushed (by family planning) and she is being used as an object of enjoyment. The girl who has come in the womb is being miscarried by family planning. Is it the honour of womankind? It is a great insult of the womankind.

If the female child is effected abortion in the pregnancy period, what will be condition later on in future? Where would you marry? How will your family flourish? You will have to pay money for the girls. My childhood is still in my mind. Those days, when the girls were born in the Vaishya family, they used to say that they had got the bag of money. But today it is said that the stone has come. Both these things are of my own experience. They used to say the bag of money to the girls because the money would be got in their marriage. Now, the same condition will come in future.

It creates a great wonder to hear that if the population increases, how will the food material be available to the people? This theory is applicable there, where the food-stock is limited and there is no possibility of more production. In such a condition it can be said—‘Brother! The food material is so scanty and the population is growing, then, how can it be shared? If the population is increasing, the production is also increasing in ratio. Where there are trees in abundance, it rains there more; If the population increases, will the production not increase? But why does dearth occur nowadays? It is because men have started eating the animals. So, there is no need of grain for the men and the grass is not needed for the animals, then, what is the need of rain? In the modern time, in the farming the living beings are killed, the locusts are killed, the mice are killed, the birds are killed, small creatures are killed so that the production may increase. But what is the cost of food grain nowadays? Previously, the creatures were not killed. Then, what was the price rate of the grain and what is the price rate of grain today? The result of violence is very horrible. If today you kill the weakers, they will also kill you to become strong. So, gentlemen! Be kind. Don’t effect abortion, don’t miscarry.

This is the rule of law that the man who disrespects the good objects, does not get the good objects again. The man who despises grain, wastes it uselessly, leaves the remainings of food, will have to starve in want of food either in this very birth or in the next birth. One who wastes the water without any proper use, will have to die of thirst for want of water. The servent who abandons the good and noble master, insults him, does not discharge his duty properly, will never get a good master. The master who disdains good servant, will never get a good servent. If fortunately you get a saint or hermit, but you neglect him, disrespect him, don’t follow his advice, you will never get good saints and hermits because you have disrespected him. If you waste this human life and don’t use it in noble deeds, you will not get the human life again. In the same way, if you abort, despise the human body, you will have to weep for the issue in the next birth. Somebody asked a saint—‘This woman is issueless, what is the reason?’ He said—‘She has committed the murder of a child in the previous birth, that is why she is issueless and she will remain issueless later on also.’ That woman remained issueless throughout her life—‘I know this, this is true, but I will not disclose her name. If anyone asserts that he who affects abortion, bears issue, then it is the fruitful result of his virtues done in the previous birth. It is sure that he will have to suffer the consequences of the great sin of affecting abortion of this birth, in the next birth. You eat the production of the last year farming, but if you don’t make effort this year for the good production, what will you eat the next year? You will have to reap what you have sowed. So, escape yourself from the sins, don’t commit severe acts of sin.’*

* To understand this subject well, the book ‘Mahapaap Se Bacho’ should be read.

 

Disadvantage of Dowry-System

Somebody asked—‘Unknowingly, I got vasectomy done, then what is it atonement?’ In this context a doctor of Jodhpur had said that the vasectomy can be opened back again, it can be set right also. Those, who have done so, have produced the child also. So, the vasectomy should be set right again by a good doctor. Its repentence is never to get it done again and never to advise others to get it done—determine so. The true repentence removes the ill-affect; it means the mistake was done by me and I will never commit such mistake in future—‘saying so, ask for pardon from the Lord and never to repeat the mistake removes the blame of sin.’

To get deliverance after reaping the fruits of all actions is impossible. If there is deliverance ever, that is by the grace of the Lord. When He pardons, we get deliverance only then. What is impossible if the God is graceful? If the repentence is with sincere heart, the Lord pardons.

The next important matter has come before, is that some people effect abortion because of the problem of dowry as if more daughters are produced, where to get dowry from to give. If the rich men show their grace by refusing to accept dowry, this great sin (abortion) can be wiped out. It is the rich men who are hungry of dowry. Injustice is also mostly done by those who have a good deal of money. So, at least be kind enough to accept my this piece of advice, don’t desire to take what belongs to others. Although we partake of meals provided by others, we put on clothes given by others, we read the books of others, we dwell in other’s house, we make journey by the fare paid by others, yet it depends on the will of the giver. We don’t desire to get from others.

I have heard from the saints. Out of five kinds of begging (Bhiksha), the ‘Madhukari Vritti’ is the best one. Why it is the best? It is because there is the pleasure of the giver. That object which is given willingly is considered to be the most pious. The object received with the desire of the receiver is not pious. Somebody asked a question to a saint before me what food is good. The saint answered—‘The food that gives more pleasure to the feeder than to him who eats, is a good meal. Where the both—one who feeds and one who eats—are joyous, such meals is of medium quality and the meal which gives pleasure only to him who eats and not to him who feeds, is the worst type of meal. If the lion eats the cow by killing, will the cow be happy? It is a heinous murder.’

If anything is obtained given willingly that is like milk but if it is obtained after demanding, it is like water, if it is obtained by pulling and hauling, it is like blood. This is what the saints say—

सहज मिले सो दूध सम, माँग लिया सो पानि।

खैंचातानी रक्त सम, यह सन्तों की बानि॥

It means that ‘where there is pulling and hauling that I will take such amount of money, it is just like to drink the blood of anyone. Such money will never do good to anyone. It may seem to be good for sometime but its result is very dreadful.’

अन्यायोपार्जितं द्रव्यं दशवर्षाणि तिष्ठति।

प्राप्ते चैकादशे वर्षे समूलं तद्विनश्यति॥

The wealth grabbed by the unjust means, stays only for ten years. If there is some just means also along with the unjust means, it may remain a little longer, but after the end of the certain period the principal amount as well as the earned amount as a whole is wasted away. It is just like, the overflow of the pond, if there is any narrow passage to pass on the overflow of the water, the pond remains full with water, if the passage is not there, the flow of the water will split out and the pond will be empty. In the same way, any person who amasses the money but does not spend in charity, is sure to become destitute, none can save him. On account of the virtuous deeds of the previous birth in excess, he may pull on well for some day, but in the last, his misery is inevitable—there is not the least doubt in it.

To hurt the heart of anyone is a great sin. ‘दिल किसीका मत दुखा, दिल खुदाका नूर है!’ The heart is the abode of God; so it is not right to hurt the feelings of any being. Cow is our mother. When we drink her milk, we, first, filter it with cloth so that any soft hair may not come into it. If a single soft hair is uprooted, the cow feels pained. What is got by us pleasantly, is called milk and what is taken with displeasure of the giver is blood. So, to demand dowry is a great sin.

There was a boy. When he grew up and became well educated, several persons approached him for his engagement but he did not accept any. Ultimately, he married to such a girl of his clan who was blind. His friends forbade him to do so, then, he said to them that he wanted to wed her, whom he might serve. He does not want to wed to take her service, but he wanted to give her service. He himself used to cook the food and wash the clothes. The issue was also born to her. Thus, his married life passed very happily. Such an occurrence is possible only in the mankind and not in the animals. The dogs play with great fondness but just the moment, the piece of bread comes before them, the quarrel starts among them. But in the same way if men start quarrelling for grabbing something for themselves, that is conducive to their downfall.

This is a remarkable matter that you cannot spend all what you earn. the entire amassed wealth will not be utilized by you; but the evils what you have done, for amassing it, will go with you; not even a small portion will be left after. If we call him wise who carries the bundle of sins for wealth amassed and left here, then who will be called block-headed? Now you feel happy but the result will be miserable. ‘पड़ेगा काम दूतों से, धरेंगे मार जूतों से’! When you have to face the Yama’s messengers, they will thrash you with shoes.’ So, kindly, consider over that what happened in the past, already happened but I will not do so again, I will never take anything by force from anyone. To take money from anyone by pressure is so great a sin as to commit robbery or theft. When will you shun such sin? What will be that day, when you will eschew sin?

The bridegroom people demand dowry that they will take so much. The bride-side cannot speak directly.
