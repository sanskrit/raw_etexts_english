## CXXIII

*To the Officer in Charge of Telegrams, Srinagar*

*April 19, 1898*.

SIR,

Please allow Miss M'cLeod \[MacLeod\] or her agent to receive any
telegrams that you have received for me and receipt the same.

Yours truly,  
VIVEKANANDA
