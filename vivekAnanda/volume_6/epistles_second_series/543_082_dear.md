## LXXXII

READING, ENGLAND,  
*4th Oct., 1895.*

DEAR— ,

. . . Purity, patience, and perseverance overcome all obstacles. All
great things must of necessity be slow. . . . 

Yours with love,

VIVEKANANDA.
