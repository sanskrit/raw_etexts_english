## XXXVII

MOUNT ABU,  
*1891*.

DEAR GOVINDA SAHAY,

You must go on with your Japa whatever direction the mind takes. Tell
Harbux that he is to begin with the Prânâyâma in the following way.

Try hard with your Sanskrit studies. 

Yours with love,

V.
