## CXXX

*To Sister Nivedita*

*3 p.m. Sunday.*  
\[*Early 1899*\]

MY DEAR MARGOT,

I am sorry I cannot come to see Dr. Mahoney[1](#fn1) — I am ill. I have
not yet broken my fast.

Have you stopped teaching my little cousin?

Yours with love,  
VIVEKANANDA

1.  [^](#fn1_1)Dr. Mahoney was the District Medical Officer who used to
