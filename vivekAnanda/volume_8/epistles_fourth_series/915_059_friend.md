## LIX

*2nd November, 1895*.

DEAR FRIEND (Mr. E. T. Sturdy),

I think you are right; we shall work on our own lines and let things
grow.

I send you the note of the lecture.

I shall come on Sunday if nothing extraordinary prevents me. 

Yours with love,

VIVEKANANDA.
