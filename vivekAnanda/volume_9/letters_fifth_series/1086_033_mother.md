## XXXIII

*To Mrs. G. W. Hale*

HOTEL BELLEVUE, EUROPEAN PLAN  
BEACON STREET, BOSTON

*12 September 1894*

DEAR MOTHER

I hope you will immediately send me over the little scrap from the
*Indian Mirror* about my Detroit lectures which I sent you.

Yours,  
VIVEKANANDA
