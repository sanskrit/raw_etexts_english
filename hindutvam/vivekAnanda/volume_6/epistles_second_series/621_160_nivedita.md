## CLX

NEW YORK,  
*2nd July, 1900*.

DEAR NIVEDITA,

. . . Mother knows, as I always say. Pray to Mother. It is hard work to
be a leader — one must crush all one's own self under the feet of the
community. . . .

Yours etc.,  
VIVEKANANDA.
